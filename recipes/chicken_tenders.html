Chicken Tender Recipe

Home

Crispy breaded fried chicken tenders made simply to have you enjoy your guilty pleasures

Ingredients!

  • 2 pounds chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Vegatable oil

Instructions!

  1. Add oil to pan on medium high heat then bread chicken
  2. Mix flour salt and pepper
  3. Another bowl for the beaten egg and water
  4. Prep chicken the set aside for 5 minutes
  5. Cook on one side for 8 to 10 minutes then swap to other one for same amount
  6. Transfer to towel then wait 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
